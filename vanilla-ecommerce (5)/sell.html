Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to sell on zoroMart

zoroMart charges a commission based on the product category, typically ranging from 5% to 20%. There are no listing fees or monthly subscription charges.

How long does the registration process take?

The registration process typically takes 2-7 business days, depending on document verification and approval.

What documents do I need to start selling?

You need PAN card, bank account details, GST certificate (if applicable), and address proof for your business.

How do I receive payments?

Payments are transferred to your bank account within 7-15 days after the order is delivered and the return period expires.
